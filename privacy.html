Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. This page explains how CheckIT handles data.

Last updated: June 2026

Information We Collect

CheckIT may collect limited information necessary to operate the app. This may include your account email (if you create an account) and claims you choose to analyze or save.

Saved Claims & Account Data

If you choose to save claims, they are stored securely so you can revisit them later. Saved claim history is associated with your account and is not shared publicly.

Images, Links, and Text Submitted

Claims submitted through text, screenshots, or links are processed in order to analyze the information and generate results. This content is used only for analysis and is not sold or shared for advertising purposes.

Third-Party Services

CheckIT uses the following third-party services to operate and improve the app: Firebase (Google) for authentication and data storage, OpenAI for claim analysis, and SerpAPI for retrieving source information. These providers may process limited data necessary to deliver these services and are bound by their own privacy policies.

Crash Reporting

CheckIT uses Firebase Crashlytics to identify and fix technical issues. This may include device information and crash logs, which help us improve app stability.

Data Security

CheckIT is designed with user privacy in mind. Reasonable security measures are used to protect stored information and prevent unauthorized access. If you delete your account, your data is permanently removed from our systems immediately.

Children's Privacy

CheckIT is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately at support@checkitapp.in.

Your Choices

You control whether to create an account, save claims, or submit information for analysis. You may delete your account and saved data at any time from within the app. Depending on your location, you may have rights under applicable privacy laws (such as the GDPR or CCPA) to access, correct, or delete your personal information.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time to reflect improvements to the app or changes in services used by CheckIT.

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact:
