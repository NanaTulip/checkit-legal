Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. This page explains how CheckIT handles data.

Last updated: April 2026

Information We Collect

CheckIT may collect limited information necessary to operate the app. This may include your account email (if you create an account) and claims you choose to analyze or save.

Saved Claims & Account Data

If you choose to save claims, they are stored securely so you can revisit them later. Saved claim history is associated with your account and is not shared publicly.

Images, Links, and Text Submitted

Claims submitted through text, screenshots, or links are processed in order to analyze the information and generate results. This content is used only for analysis and is not sold or shared for advertising purposes.

Third-Party Services

CheckIT may rely on trusted third-party services to help analyze claims and provide supporting information. These services may process limited claim data in order to generate results.

Data Security

CheckIT is designed with user privacy in mind. Reasonable security measures are used to protect stored information and prevent unauthorized access.

Your Choices

You control whether to create an account, save claims, or submit information for analysis. You may delete your account and saved data at any time from within the app.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time to reflect improvements to the app or changes in services used by CheckIT.
